# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Power BI** to help stakeholders monitor key business metrics, evaluate sales performance, and identify actionable insights. The dashboard enables users to explore sales trends, regional performance, customer segments, and product profitability through interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses need a centralized view of their sales performance to answer questions such as:

- Which regions generate the highest sales?
- Which product categories are the most profitable?
- Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
- How have sales changed over time?
- Which products drive the highest sales?

This dashboard was designed to answer these questions and support data-driven decision-making.

## Dashboard Features

The report consists of three interactive pages:

### 1. Sales Dashboard
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Discount
- Sales by Region
- Sales by State (Map)
- Sales Trend
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Profit by Category
- Top Selling Products

### 2. Product Analysis
Provides insights into:
- Product performance
- Category performance
- Sub-category analysis
- Product profitability

### 3. Customer Insights
Provides insights into:
- Customer segments
- Regional customer distribution
- Customer purchasing behaviour
- Sales contribution by customer group

## Key Insights

- The **West** region generated the highest sales.
- **Technology** recorded the highest overall profit.
- The **Consumer** segment contributed the largest share of sales.
- Sales increased steadily throughout the analysis period.
- A small number of products generated a significant proportion of total sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Continue investing in high-performing regions while developing strategies to improve underperforming areas.
- Focus marketing efforts on profitable product categories.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies for high-value customer segments.
- Monitor discount levels to maintain profitability.
